Detailed Description

Creates a Yaw-Pitch-Roll camera that integrates into the Bullet Physics world. The camera is an extension of the btRigidBody class.

)

Constructor.

Position is at the origin, the view vector is pointing down the negative z-axis, up is the positive y direction. Also sets attributes to allow the camera to move about and interact in the physics scene without rolling, gravity, or bouncing.
